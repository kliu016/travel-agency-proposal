‚ö° Current Automations

These automations run automatically to keep the team informed:

TTA - Air Week Ahead
Scheduled: Runs every week to find upcoming flights and send summary email
TTA - New Client Notification
Triggered: When a new client record is created, sends notification email
TTA - VVIP Hotel (Transient) - 7 Day Outlook
Scheduled: Daily check for VIP guests arriving in next 7 days
TTA - Group Hotel - 7 Day Outlook
Scheduled: Daily check for group arrivals in next 7 days
